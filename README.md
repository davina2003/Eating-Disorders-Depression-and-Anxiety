# Eating-Disorders-Depression-and-Anxiety
This repository consists of two datasets: Dataset 1 and Datset 2 originally extracted from the OSF.

Dataset 1: "https://osf.io/697fn"
Consists of data from 231 countries/ regions from 1990 to 2017, with the variables: eating disorders, depression and anxiety. The variables have percentages of the rate of mentaal health disorder.

Dataset 2: "https://osf.io/4t35j/"
Consists of data from students (N=180) investigating the relationship between Asian identity, neuroticism, gender and depression on eating disorders.


The relationship between depression and anxiety on eating disorders was investigated in Dataset 1 and the relationship between depression and eating disorders was investigated in Dataset 2
